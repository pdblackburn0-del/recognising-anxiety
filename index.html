What is Anxiety?

Anxiety is a natural human response to stress, worry, or feeling threatened, but it can sometimes become overwhelming. It often shows up as persistent nervousness, restlessness, or fear about everyday situations, even when thereâ€™s no immediate danger. People may experience physical symptoms like a fast heartbeat, stomach discomfort, or trouble concentrating. While occasional anxiety is normal, ongoing or intense anxiety can affect daily life. The good news is that anxiety is common, understandable, and manageable with the right support and coping strategies.
